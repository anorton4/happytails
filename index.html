About Us

How our "tail" began and where we are now.

  • How We Started

    Our passion for rescue dogs has been a driving force in the start of Happy Tails. Our very own pups are rescues and they have completely changed our lives for the better.

  • 2016-2018

    Founded in 2018, we wanted to start a rescue that not only places dogs in new homes, but focuses on finding the best fit for families and to provide continuing support for them.

  • 2018-Now

    We started from rescuing about 20 dogs a year to now several hundred, thanks to our amazing staff, fosters, and volunteers.
