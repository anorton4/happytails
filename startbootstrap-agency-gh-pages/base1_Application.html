About Us

How our "tail" began and where we are now.

  • Our Humble Beginnings

    Our passion for rescue dogs has been a driving force in the start of Happy Tails. Our very own pups are rescues and they have completely changed our lives for the better.

  • 2016-2018

    Happy Tails is Born

    Founded in 2018, we wanted to start a rescue that not only places dogs in new homes, but focuses on finding the best fit for families and to provide continuing support for them.

  • 2018-Now

    We started from rescuing about 20 dogs a year to now several hundred, thanks to our amazing staff, fosters, and volunteers.

  • Weight: 40 lbs
  • Energy Level: Medium
  • Temperament: Good with kids and other dogs

Garrus

German Shepherd/Labrador Mix

Garrus loves to run, chase squirrels, and give doggie kisses.

  • Age: 5 years old
  • Weight: 50 lbs
  • Energy Level: Medium
  • Temperament Good with older kids. Garrus should be the only dog in the household.

Liara

Border Collie mix.

Liara is very smart and has lots of energy for playtime!

  • Age: 2 years old
  • Weight: 35 lbs
  • Energy Level: High
  • Temperament: Good with older kids and would prefer to be in a household with another dog.

Zym

Yorkshire Terrier

Zym loves to snuggle on the couch after the zoomies!

  • Age: 3 years
  • Weight: Small
  • Energy Level: Low/Medium
  • Temperament: Great family dog, but barks at other dogs on walks which just requires some corrective training!

Mordin

Jack Russell Terrier Mix

Mordin is super curious and goes where his nose leads him!

  • Age: 4 years
  • Weight: Small
  • Energy Level: High
  • Temperament: Would be great in a household that loves the outdoors! Good with older kids and other dogs.

Clara

American Foxhound/Bulldog Mix

Clara is very gentle, loving, and sweet. She'll always want to be by your side!

  • Age: 2 years
  • Weight: 60 lbs
  • Energy Level: Low/Medium
  • Temperament: Great family dog who loves kids! Clara has separation anxiety so she needs an understanding family.
